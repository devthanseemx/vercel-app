Cookies Policy

Last updated: December 2025

This Cookies Policy explains how QuickHire LK (a final year project) uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our platform. It explains what these technologies are and why we use them for this academic demonstration.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and provide a better user experience. In this project, cookies are used to demonstrate modern web development practices.

2. How We Use Cookies

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the platform to function:

  • Session management for logged-in users
  • Security and authentication
  • Basic platform functionality

Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance your experience:

  • Remembering your preferences
  • Language and location settings
  • Theme preferences (if applicable)

Analytics Cookies

For academic purposes, we may use analytics cookies to understand how users interact with the platform. This helps in evaluating the project's effectiveness and user experience design.

3. Third-Party Cookies

This project may use third-party services for development and demonstration purposes. These services may set their own cookies according to their privacy policies.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:

  • Your browser settings
  • Clearing cookies regularly
  • Disabling specific types of cookies
  • Using private/incognito browsing mode

5. Cookie Categories

6. Updates to This Policy

This Cookies Policy may be updated as the project evolves. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

7. Contact Information

If you have questions about our use of cookies or this policy, please contact us through the appropriate academic channels.

Academic Project Notice
