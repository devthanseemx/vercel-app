Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how QuickHire LK (a final year project) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our platform. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Username and email address
  • Profile information and skills
  • Location data (for job matching purposes)
  • Communication preferences

Usage Information

We collect information about how you interact with our platform, including pages visited, features used, and time spent on the platform for academic analysis purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

As an academic project, we use collected information to:

  • Demonstrate platform functionality
  • Analyze user experience and interface design
  • Improve platform features and usability
  • Conduct academic research on job marketplace platforms

3. Information Sharing

As this is an academic project, your information is not shared with third parties for commercial purposes. Data may be used in academic presentations or reports in an anonymized format to protect user privacy.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, as this is a project environment, please do not share sensitive personal or financial information.

5. Data Retention

Project data will be retained for the duration of the academic program and may be used for educational purposes. Users can request data deletion by contacting the project team.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of data collection

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us through the appropriate academic channels.

Academic Project Notice:

This platform is developed for educational purposes. All data handling practices are designed to meet academic standards and protect user privacy during the demonstration and evaluation of this project.
